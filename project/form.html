Frequently asked questions for choosing the type of account

Can I convert my individual account into an institution account?

Of course, young wizard! You can start with a personal account, learn, and who knows if you too will wish to train other young wizards during your learning journey. Knowledge and wisdom should be bestowed upon every adventurer who desires it. By doing so, you will also grow, and your magical power along with you.

What happens if I want to join a school?

You stand before a grand community of wizards. A messenger owl will be sent to the institution you desire, and you will be given a name in their records. From there, you will be part of a great community of wizards and sorcerers eager to help you.

Will an individual account prevent me from obtaining certificates?

Absolutely not! Whether you are part of an institution account or an individual account, the holy grail will grant you the wisdom you need and a scroll with your name that demonstrates to the world the realms, scrolls, and oracles you have conquered. Do not fear, begin your journey today.
