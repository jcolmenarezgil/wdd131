Site purpose

The website offers education plans through the membership process, as well as educational plans for educational institutions. It has a registration form where students can access an educational sample that records their progress. ⭐⭐⭐

It includes a blog of student's experience in the academy as well as applicable technologies in programming, as well as forums for discussion on programming topics.

Scenarios

What is the best programming language for 4-6 year olds to learn?

Is there a tracking tool to help students measure their progress in programming?
